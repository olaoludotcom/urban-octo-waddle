Importex Investments Limited is an importer and wholesale distributor of commodities and fast moving consumer goods in Nigeria.

We specialise in international trade, sales and distribution of goods and commodities, acting as Manufacturers' Representatives and Sole Distributors for overseas manufacturers. We leverage on our extensive knowledge and deep understanding of the Nigerian market to assist foreign companies in accessing the fertile and vast but complex Nigerian terrain.

Importex Investments Limited is a member company of Timeless Holdings Limited.
 

We build and maintain our relationships on avowed principles of absolute loyalty to our principals and business associates on one hand and on the other, delivery of excellent products at best prices at all times to our numerous customers .

This way, we have succeeded in building a profile of a trusted name and brand, one that our international suppliers and local market contacts have come to cherish for efficiency in operations and reliability in delivery.

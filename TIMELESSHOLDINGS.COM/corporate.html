Group Mission :::

Our Mission in Timeless Holdings is to focus on high-yield investment opportunities where potential returns will exceed our cost of capital.

Group Structure :::

Currectly, the group has varying degrees of investments in the following companies either as associates or subsidiaries.

 

Investment Philosophy :::

Timeless holdings invests in businesses that deliver superior earnings with minimal investment risks. This involves probing into both local and international market opportunities and making acquisition of meaningful interests in companies that have sound management and the ability to generate strong cash returns and growth.

Timeless holdings develops by building strategic coalitions on a partnership basis, creating value in ventures. The ultimate aim is to ensure excellent returns on investments and consistent capital growth.

 

Ownership and Management :::

Timeless Holdings Limited is owned by a group of young and dynamic Nigerian professionals of impeccable integrity who have distinguished themselves in their respective career.

Key Management Personnel :::

Yinka Suleman

Managing Director/Chief Executive Officer

Yinka holds the overall responsibility for the Group.

Yinka has over twenty years of finance, accounting, risk management and consulting experience. He has been at different times an Audit, Manager, Bank’s head of internal control, Bank’s head of risk management, general manager of a finance and stock broking firm and managing partner of a management consulting firm.

Academically, Yinka holds an HND (Ilaro Polytechnic), an MBA (Lagos State University) and an M.Sc (Westminster University, London). He is an Associate of The Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria and The Chartered Institute of Taxation of Nigeria. Yinka is widely read and extensively travelled.

 

Daisi Omidiji

Chief Finance & Strategy Officer

Daisi drives the corporate strategy of the Group using strategic financial management as a tool.

He brings to the company a very diverse and broad based educational and professional experience covering Banking, Accounting, Tax Management, Corporate Finance, Management Consulting, Corporate Planning and Strategy.

Over a span of eighteen years, Daisi was at different points an investment banker, multinational corporate treasurer, multinational corporate planner & strategist, management consultant & training specialist, international trade, finance & development professional.

Academically, he holds a B. Sc (Accounting), Obafemi Awolowo University & an MBA (Finance), University of Benin. He is a Fellow of the Certified Cost Management Institute of Nigeria, a Fellow of The Chartered Institute of Taxation of Nigeria and an Associate of The Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria.

Daisi has attended many Management and Executive Management courses within and outside Nigeria and has also written and delivered many papers on various aspects of finance, strategy and general management.
